What is the vital capacity calculator?
The vital capacity calculator is a tool that computes the volume of air that flows through your lungs. How to calculate vital capacity?
To calculate vital capacity you should use the following formula:
vital capacity (male) = height * (27.63 - 0.112 * age)
vital capacity (female) = height * (21.78 - 0.101 * age)
Why use vital capacity calculator?
The vital capacity calculator is used in order to measure the volume of air that flows through your lungs.
